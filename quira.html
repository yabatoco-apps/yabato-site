Quira

Habit Tracker

Core idea

A calm habit tracker built around visible progress.

Quira gives one clear answer every time you open it: how far you have come since the moment you decided to change.

Live habit streaks shown down to seconds
Goal duration targets with clean progress bars
Reward milestones, savings, and motivational achievements

Features

Everything you need to see progress clearly.

Quit or build habits

Choose whether you want to stop a habit or create a new routine.

Track streaks

See your progress down to seconds with a simple live counter.

Set goals

Create duration goals and follow progress with a clean percentage bar.

Estimate savings

Add a daily cost and Quira estimates how much you have saved over time.

Motivational milestones

Unlock achievement cards as your habit journey continues.

Private by design

Your habit data is stored locally on your device.

Preview

A calm interface for daily tracking.

How it works

Start with a habit, then let Quira make your progress visible.

Choose whether you want to quit a habit or build a new one, then select a counter or goal-based tracker. You can add a daily cost to estimate money saved, set optional reminders for positive habits, and follow your streak, goal progress, milestones, and overall statistics from the main tabs.
