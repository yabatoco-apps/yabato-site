Monevia

Money Clarity

Core idea

A personal finance app built around one direct question.

“How much money do I really have left this month?”

Features

Simple tools for understanding your month.

Salary and payday

Set your monthly salary and payday to see how many days remain until your next income cycle.

Extra income

Add freelance work, allowance, bonuses, or any extra income that affects your month.

Expenses and debts

Track food, groceries, transport, subscriptions, credit cards, loans, and installments.

Duration options

Create fixed records or expenses that last for 1 month, 3 months, or 6 months.

Safe daily limit

See your estimated remaining money and safe daily spending limit for the current period.

Language and currency

Use Turkish or English, choose a currency, and work with exchange conversion when needed.

How it works

Designed for quick financial awareness, not complicated accounting.

On first launch, Monevia asks for your monthly salary, payday, and at least one expense. After setup, the home screen shows your remaining money, safe daily spending limit, and days until payday. Income and expense tabs let you adjust salary, add extra income, and record expenses or debts with notes and duration options.
