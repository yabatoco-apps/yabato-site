Quira Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 28, 2026

Quira: Habit Tracker (“Quira”, “the app”, “we”, “us”) is developed by Yabato Co. This Privacy Policy explains how Quira handles information when you use the app.

1. Information Stored by the App

Quira is designed to work locally on your device. We do not operate a server, account system, or backend database for storing user data.

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Your optional name
  • Your selected language, currency, and theme preferences
  • Habits you create or select
  • Habit type, goal duration, streak information, reset history, reminder time, and daily cost if you choose to enter it
  • Achievement progress based on your habit tracking

2. How This Information Is Used

The information stored in the app is used only to provide app features, such as:

  • Showing your habit streaks
  • Calculating progress toward goals
  • Estimating saved money based on daily cost
  • Displaying motivational achievement milestones
  • Showing simple statistics about your progress
  • Sending local reminders if you enable them
  • Personalizing the app experience

3. Local Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device. Quira does not send your habit data, name, cost information, or progress data to our servers.

If you delete the app or clear app data from your device settings, locally stored data may be deleted.

4. Notifications

Quira may send local notifications if you enable reminders for habit-building activities. Notifications are scheduled on your device and are used only to remind you at the time you select.

5. Achievement Milestones

Quira may display motivational achievement milestones based on your habit progress. These milestones are for motivation and general self-tracking only and do not replace professional advice.

6. Third-Party Services

Quira does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external tracking services.

7. Children’s Privacy

Quira is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

9. Your Choices

You can:

  • Edit or delete habits inside the app
  • Disable reminders
  • Change language, currency, and theme preferences
  • Clear app data from your device settings
  • Delete the app from your device

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: yabato.co@gmail.com

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

By using Quira, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
